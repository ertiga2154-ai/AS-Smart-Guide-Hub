🩸 Diabetes / High Sugar Care

🔴 High Sugar-e Ki Khaben Na: Chini, mishti, batasha, cola drink, boro aam, kolar moton high glucose fruit, boro alu ar moidar porota porat pori bondho rakhun।
🟢 High Sugar-e Ki Khaben: Korolla-r jush, dherosh bhejano jol, oats, daliya, sosa, labra torkari ar bhat er poriman komiye ruti khoroch korun।
🏃‍♂️ Ki Korben & Korben Na: Khali paye math-e hatben na, protidin sakal-e jore 30 min hatun, rater ranna 8:30 tar modhe complete korun। Insulin checkup bad deben na।

🫀 High Blood Pressure (BP)

❌ Avoid: Kaacha noon (Raw Salt) khabar upar theke ekdom bondho, khashir mangsho, papor bhaja, achhar ar dhumpan full bondho।
✅ Allowed: Rosun-er koa, tok doi, lemon water, kola (potassium thake), oga shak-sobji।

🧪 High Cholesterol Care

❌ Avoid: Dalda, bonoshpoti, ghie bhaja porota, mangsher chorbi, dim-er kusum ar fast-food er dokaner teler tel-bhaja।
✅ Allowed: Isabgul-er bhushi, methi bheja jol, omar methi gura, rui-katla macher patla jhol ar oliver oil use।

🦋 Thyroid Management

❌ Avoid: Badhakopi, fulkopi, olkopi, soya chunk/soyabean er torkari, ar atirikto caffeine cha-coffee single day te bad deya bhalo।
✅ Allowed: Iodiene jukto lobon, badam, mach, dim er shada ongsho ar jodu niyomito thyroid test kora।
